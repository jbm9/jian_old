Overall Impression

(Quicksilver was the first novel I picked up after a long time without reading any novels, so my impressions might be skewwed.)

This was an overall fun read, which I thoroughly enjoyed once I accepted skimming its. There were a few things that bothered me, though. The only one worth mentioning directly was Newton's constantly foreshadowing Einstein. It's just bad form, in my humble opinion, and felt like a belabored in-joke among those who would find it "ironic."
